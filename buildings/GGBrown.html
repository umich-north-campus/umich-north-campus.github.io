George G. (GG) Brown Laboratories

History

A group of engineering buildings were named after George Granger Brown in 1957, who was known for his career accomplishments. The building would include a laboratory for fluids engineering, another for materials, metallurgy and structures, and a lab to study highways. Through several renovations, the building has been expanded and modernized over the years.
