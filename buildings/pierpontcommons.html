Pierpont Commons

History

Pierpont Commons is the student union on North Campus. A wide range of services are provided, and several restaurants are located inside the Pierpont Commons. Panda Express provides Chinese food including orange chicken, beef with broccoli, and Kung Bao chicken. Hibachi-San provides Japanese food including sushi and bowls, and bubble milk tea is also available. Fireside Roast and Café provides coffee, snacks, salad, burger, chicken wings, hotdogs…, including healthy and grab-and-go entries.

Fun Facts

There is a Barnes and Noble bookstore providing stationery, notebooks, and even UM clothes. Sometimes you can find nice discounts for the UM clothes they sell.

There is a computer store where you can buy laptop, headphone, computer, USB cables, smart watch, iPad, iPod, and all kinds of desktop accessories.

There is a BLUE market where you can find pizza, drinks, and all kinds of snacks.

There is a piano for anyone who wants to practice or play for people passing by the commons. There are computers nearby, so sometimes you can enjoy others’ performance while working there.

There are national flags hanging from the ceiling of the corridor of the Pierpont Commons.
